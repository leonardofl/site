We are approaching our research about “how to organize teams and departments for managing infrastructure in the context of continuous delivery” by interviewing industry professionals working with products delivered by a continuous delivery flow.

Our interviews follow this protocol:

  • We pose a series of questions to be freely answered by the interviewed.
  • The focus is on the recent experience of the interviewed.
  • We expect the interview to take from 40 to 60 minutes.
  • If the interview is online, we prefer to chat by video.
  • The interview is recorded for after-analysis.
  • We will keep the record under secrecy.
  • The interview analysis will be conducted by our research team: Leonardo Leite (Ph.D. student at USP), prof. Fabio Kon (USP), and prof. Paulo Meirelles (Unifesp).
  • We will send the research results to the interviewed people.
  • The publication, originated from interviews, will anonymize interviewed people and their organizations.
